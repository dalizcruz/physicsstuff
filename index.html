Newton's Laws of Motion

Isaac Newton was born in England on December 25, 1642. Newton developed the work of Galileo Gagilei on three important laws that consider force and mass. These laws are known to be "Newton's laws of motion" and provide a basic understanding of the effect forces have on an object.
